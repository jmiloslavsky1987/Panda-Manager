**RiskEditModal.tsx:**
Props: `{ risk: Risk, trigger: React.ReactNode }`

Form fields inside DialogContent:
- `severity`: `<select>` — options: low, medium, high, critical (editable — per CONTEXT.md)
- `status`: text input (free-form: 'open', 'mitigated', 'resolved', 'closed', 'accepted' — keep as text not enum)
